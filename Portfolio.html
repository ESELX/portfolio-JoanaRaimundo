Digital Representation

In digital representation we work in 3D modeling in the blender program. It is where you proceed to create products or characters with their respective backgrounds, textures and adequate lighting.

Product Design

In product design we developed several models and prototypes, with the aim of creating innovative, practical and uncomplicated products, always thinking about its production process and its costs paying the utmost attention to detail.

Graphic Design

In graphic design, the goal is to create a design that is balanced and pleasing to the eye at, combining colors, fonts, illustrations, vectors ... The programs used are Illustrator, Photoshop and inDesign.

Materials for Design

In materials for design we intend to discover new materials and explore their different applications and utilities in works with these, giving emphasis to materials outside their usual application. One of the developed works promoted the collection of several materials and their subsequent identification in order to create a library of materials open to all.
